Pricing & Longevity

Why choose Smile Bright Dental for orthodontic treatment?

We offer multiple orthodontic options (metal, ceramic, lingual, and clear aligners), UK-trained dental surgeons with great chairside rapport, and transparent plans with no hidden costs.

What is the cost of orthodontic treatment?

No fixed price—plans are tailored to your case, schedule and budget. If you proceed with a full plan, we offset the initial consultation fee within your treatment cost.

Do you guarantee the results?

Medicine can’t promise absolutes, but we stand by our work. As long as you stay engaged with your plan, we’ll continue until we reach the agreed outcome—at no additional charge.

What payment options do you have?

Downpayment + monthly instalments are available. We accept major credit cards, NETS and bank transfers.

How long do orthodontic treatments last?

Case-dependent. With proper aftercare and retainer use, your smile should remain corrected.

Process & Procedures

Does orthodontic treatment hurt?

Modern techniques keep discomfort minimal and manageable. Most patients are surprised by how smooth treatment feels.

What special care will I need during treatment?

Good hygiene and regular reviews (typically every ~6 weeks). This is different from your routine 6-month dental cleaning—both are important.

What treatment options are there?

We’ll assess whether metal braces, clear/ceramic braces, or Invisalign® aligners best suit your goals and lifestyle.

  • Metal braces — durable, widely used, excellent control.
  • Clear/ceramic braces — tooth-coloured brackets for a subtler look.
  • Invisalign® clear aligners — removable trays planned for staged tooth movement.
What problems can arise during treatment?

Teeth may move faster/slower than planned. Regular monitoring allows timely adjustments and helps protect tooth roots.

Will the process take a long time?

New cases commonly take 12–24 months. Refinements/adjustments after prior treatment typically take 4–8 months.

What technology is used?

We use CBCT scanning (where appropriate), 3-shape intra-oral scanning and 3D printing to plan precise, efficient care.

Patient Transfers

Do you accept orthodontic transfer patients?

Yes. We’re experienced continuing treatment started elsewhere. At consult, we’ll estimate remaining duration and explain payment options. We can liaise with your previous clinic for records if needed.

What if I leave Singapore before my treatment is completed?

We’ll prepare the necessary records and the AAO transfer form to help your new orthodontist continue smoothly with minimal disruption.

CHAS Dental Subsidies

What is the CHAS Dental Subsidy Scheme?

The Community Health Assist Scheme (CHAS) provides dental subsidies to eligible Singaporeans, including Pioneer and Merdeka Generation groups, to reduce out-of-pocket costs for essential dental care.

How does the CHAS subsidy process work?

1) Check eligibility — have a valid CHAS card (tier determines subsidy).
2) Choose a CHAS-accredited clinic — Smile Bright Dental participates in CHAS.
3) Book your appointment — let us know you’ll use CHAS.
4) Present your CHAS card — physical or digital (Singpass).
5) Receive treatment — subsidy is applied directly.
6) Pay the balance — after the subsidy deduction.

How much subsidy can I receive?

Subsidy levels depend on your card type. CHAS Blue, CHAS Orange, Pioneer Generation and Merdeka Generation cards are eligible (CHAS Green is not). For current tables and criteria, see the official CHAS website.
